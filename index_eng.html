Advantages and features

Decentralize

The transaction process between the people and people will be completed without the intervention of a third party

Open and transparency

Not referring to the publicity of user data, but all transaction records.

Consensus algorithm

On the Internet, you can trust the operation and mechanism of computers instead of trusting people.

Data cannot be modified

Through the encryption method of cryptography, the data created before will not be modified.

Anonymity

The transaction process will be recorded with the public key, and the private key will be saved by itself.

Distributed ledger Technology(DLT)

Decentralized means that an asset database can be shared regardless of country, location, and the ledger is to record any financial and legal assets and each person has one.
